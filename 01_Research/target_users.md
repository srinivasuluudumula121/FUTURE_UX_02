# Target Users

## Primary Users

### Working Professionals
People who want quick appointment booking without waiting in queues.

### College Students
Young users who frequently book grooming services and prefer convenience.

### Returning Customers
Customers who already know their preferred barber and want fast booking.

### First-Time Customers
Users unfamiliar with salon staff who need recommendations for the best barber based on selected service.

## User Goals

Users want to:

- Avoid waiting in salons
- Book appointments quickly
- Select preferred services
- Choose a barber if needed
- Get suitable recommendations
- Easily reschedule appointments

## User Pain Points

Current frustrations include:

- Long waiting times
- Uncertainty about barber availability
- Manual booking confusion
- No appointment visibility
- Poor time management
