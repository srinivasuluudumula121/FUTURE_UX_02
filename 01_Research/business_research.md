# Business Research

## Business Name
Bhai Looks Saloon

## Location
Pamuru

## Business Type
Men's / Unisex Salon Appointment Booking App

## Current Booking Process
Currently, appointments are managed manually through:

- Walk-ins
- Phone communication
- In-person booking

No digital appointment system exists.

## Services Offered

- Haircut
- Beard Styling
- Hair Spa
- Facial

## Pricing

| Service | Price |
|----------|--------|
| Haircut | ₹299 |
| Beard Styling | ₹149 |

## Business Problems

Current manual booking process may lead to:

- Missed appointments
- Scheduling confusion
- Customer waiting time
- Poor customer convenience
- No structured appointment management

## Customer Reviews Summary

The salon received mostly positive reviews highlighting:

- Friendly owner
- Hygienic environment
- Skilled staff
- Good customer experience
- Relaxing atmosphere
- Well-trained barbers

## Key Insights

Customers value:

- Hygiene
- Good service quality
- Skilled staff
- Friendly behavior
- Less waiting time

This research helped identify the opportunity to improve the customer booking experience through a mobile-first appointment booking system.
